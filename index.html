What I'm Proudest Of

Kero Kero Collector!

Personal Project

Idle-Clicker mobile game in which you collect frogs and design a garden. Created in Unity Game Engine, written in C#

I designed and coded this game from the ground-up

The player owns a garden in which they can place furniture items. These furniture items attract frogs.
The player is able to collect the frogs that visit their garden, and these frogs will bring the player in-game currency.
This currency can be used to purchase more furniture that will attract more frogs.
